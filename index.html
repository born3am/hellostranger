...Hello Stranger!

One word, several ways

Did you know that it is possible to say hello to someone in thousands of ways?

Human beings so far identified 7.099 different languages or dialects.

Moreover, each language also has multiple ways to say hello, often changing according to its region.

In Germany for example, we have: Hallo, Moin, Moin-Moin, Na, Halli-Hallo, Gude, Hallöchen, Servus, Jo!...

This website aims to identify as many hellos as possible around the globe.
